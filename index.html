Team Scorpion


Team Scorpion is a student run group of highly motivated undergraduate students from the Amrita University. The team’s purpose is to explore exciting new technology, learn critical skills, and promote the field of robotics through entering a variety of competitions. Unlike many other student teams, the Robotics Team is not formed around any one particular contest. Rather, the team prefers to continually evolve through finding exciting new opportunities for challenge.

Humanitarian Technology (HuT) Labs

The dedicated Research lab, HuT Labs - ( Humanitarian Technology Labs) mainly focusing on Health Care, Robotics, Human Aided Devices, and Embedded Sensor Networks was established in 2012. Within a year of establishment, HuT Labs carried out significant research works with more than 10 research paper publications and quite national and international recognitions.




Paripreksya

Hi, I am Paripreksya, the first version of the Multi Terrain Rescue Robot made by the Team Scorpion.

BTW, Amma named me as "Paripreksya".


Features

Cost Effective

The bot is designed and made completely from scratch. Every part of the robot is designed and manufactured.

Interactive and Plain sailing control

User friendly control interface for interactive control of the robot.

Multi Terrain

The robot is designed in such way that it can travel on any terrain, may be a plain or a plateau.

Semi autonomous ARM

The ARM of the robot is designed in such a way that it can perform many things like carrying objects from one place to other..
